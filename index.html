FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Have more questions? Talk to our team →

How do you calculate the moving cost?

Cost depends on volume of goods, distance between cities, type of packing, and floor/elevator access. We offer a free survey and a fixed quote — no surprises later.

How many days does an intercity move take?

Local moves within Karachi are completed in 1 day. Intercity moves typically take 2–5 days depending on distance, with real‑time tracking updates.

Is my shipment insured?

Yes. All shipments are covered by comprehensive transit insurance at no extra cost.

Can I track my shipment?

Absolutely. You'll get a tracking link with real‑time GPS updates from pickup to delivery.

Do you help with unpacking and setup?

Yes, our crew will unpack boxes, place furniture and reassemble beds/tables at your new home.

What payment methods do you accept?

Cash, bank transfer, JazzCash, EasyPaisa and cheque. 30% advance at booking, balance on delivery.
